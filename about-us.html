Established in 1971,Chariox tyre is part of a large family of brands manufactured by CONVERSE tyres,one of the most trusted and time-tested names in the business.Chariox tyre is an innovation-driven company strives to be a leader and innovator in the tyre market,with state-of-the-art technology and design features.Chariox tyres manufactures the most advanced tyres,offering the toughest off road tyres and engineering innovations on the market.

Refined by logo

The Chariox roots are in the Milan,Italy where the horse has long been a traditional symbol in the region.Though the style and references in the logo have changed throughthe years,the inclusion of the horse has remained.

The new identity brings alive the core attributes of performance and Design,reflecting Chariox's innovation and success throughout its history.Its tyres have won multiple consumer tyre tests due to the best-in-class offroad and great designs.The spirit of the new identity echoes Chariox's strengths and is based on the brand idea 'refined by logo'.
